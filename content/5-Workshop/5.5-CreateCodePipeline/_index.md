2. In the Create new pipeline console choose **Build custom pipeline**:
   ![image2](/image2.png)

   ![image3](/image3.png)

   ![image4](/image4.png)

- Choose **AWS CodeBuild**
- Choose project created

![image5](/image5.png)

6. Choose skip test stage
7. Choose skip deploy stage
