1. Open the [Amazon ECR](https://ap-northeast-1.console.aws.amazon.com/ecr/get-started?region=ap-northeast-1)
2. In the navigation pane, choose **Repositories**, then click **Create repository**:
   ![image1](/5-workshop/5.3-createrepository/image1.png)
3. In the Create repository console:

- Enter name Repository
- Click Create
  ![image2](/5-workshop/5.3-createrepository/image2.png)
